What Is Semantic SEO?

Semantic SEO is the practice of optimizing content based on search intent, entities, topical relevance, and natural language processing (NLP). It helps search engines understand what your content actually means — not just which keywords it contains.

  • Entity recognition & relationship mapping
  • Contextual keyword usage
  • Intent-based content structuring
  • Topical authority development
